About the Project

Messenger App is a real-time web-based chat application that brings public, private, and group messaging into a single, intuitive interface. Built for registered users who need flexible, reliable communication, it eliminates the hassle of juggling multiple platforms by centralizing every conversation in one place. Whether you're chatting with a crowd or sending a direct message, Messenger keeps you connected instantly and securely.
